H-Factor Indexes

To build an index, we start with one of S&P Dow Jones Indexes. We then develop an investment methodology which is incorporated in a rule book. These documents are forwarded to S&P for validation and history calculation. On a quarterly basis we supply H-Factor to S&P who rebalance the indexes.

These S&P Dow Jones calculated indexes then form the basis of our SMAs and all of our investment products.
